Special omelette recipe

Below is the recipe for preparing a special omelette.

Ingredients:

  • Cooking oil
  • Onions
  • Eggs
  • Saussage
  • Red/green bell pepper
  • Cheese

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Fillings: Onions, saussage, red/green pepper, etc.
  2. Whisk the Eggs: Crack the eggs into a medium bowl. Add a pinch of salt and, if desired, 1 teaspoon of water. Whisk vigorously until the eggs are light and fluffy.
  3. Heat the Pan: Place a small non-stick pan over medium heat and melt the olive oil or butter.
  4. Pour in the Eggs and Cook:
  5. Add Fillings and Fold: When the eggs are mostly set, sprinkle the cheese and your prepared fillings (if using) over one half of the omelette. Gently fold the unfilled side over the filling with a spatula.
  6. Serve
